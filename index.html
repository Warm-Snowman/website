way of the man

Marred is a man's journey, with pain and suffering. Thus has it been so since the Dawn of time. For when his sweat and blood sacralizes the ground, shall it flourish from which his brother kind will eat.
He is the shepherd and the soldier of his family, for in his strength lies the lives of his those beloved. He is the sword of god, the harbringer of his righteous fury, for the land that he protects shall it bare forth harvest of tomorrow.
